# Skill: CapStation Brand Identity

Use this skill whenever you work on visual design, colors, UI mood, logo usage, copy tone, or brand consistency for CapStation.

## Brand summary

CapStation is a premium Saudi/Gulf mobility platform for captains/drivers. The product should feel like a modern tech startup that helps captains get better opportunities, manage their journey, and improve income.

## Logo interpretation

The logo combines:
- Captain/driver icon.
- Road and movement.
- City/growth columns.
- Green and gold colors.

This means the website should visually communicate:
- Trust.
- Movement.
- Income growth.
- Smart operations.
- Saudi/Gulf market friendliness.
- Professional driver services.

## Usage rules

- Use green as the main brand color.
- Use gold only for premium highlights, rewards, CTAs, small accents, and active details.
- Use dark green/black for premium sections.
- Use cream backgrounds to make Arabic content feel warm and high-end.
- Avoid using too many colors.
- Avoid generic blue SaaS styling.
- Avoid childish icons or cheap gradients.

## Design style

The UI should be:
- Premium, clean, and spacious.
- Rounded but not too playful.
- Smooth with intentional motion.
- Arabic-first.
- Strong enough to present to investors or partners.

## Typography direction

Use a modern Arabic font stack. Recommended:
- Arabic: `Tajawal`, `IBM Plex Sans Arabic`, or `Cairo`.
- English: `Inter`, `Manrope`, or system sans.

Prefer loading fonts locally or through a reliable setup. Keep performance in mind.

## Component feel

Buttons:
- Primary: green background, white text, subtle shadow.
- Secondary: white/transparent with green border.
- Premium CTA: green-to-dark gradient with a small gold detail.

Cards:
- White/cream surfaces.
- Soft border using brand green opacity.
- Subtle shadow.
- Hover movement of 4–8px max.

Sections:
- Alternate between light, cream, and dark premium sections.
- Use road/path shapes and route dots as brand visuals.
